Bleach

Follow Ichigo Kurosaki as he becomes a Soul Reaper to defend the world from dark spirits called Hollows. Action-packed and full of thrilling battles!

Main Characters

  • Ichigo Kurosaki: A high school student with the unique ability to see ghosts. After an encounter with a Soul Reaper, Ichigo gains their powers and takes on the responsibility of protecting the living world from Hollows.
  • Rukia Kuchiki: A noble Soul Reaper who introduces Ichigo to the world of the Soul Society. Her actions in transferring her powers to Ichigo set the story in motion.
  • Orihime Inoue: A kind-hearted friend of Ichigo with a hidden spiritual power that allows her to heal wounds and reverse events. She is deeply loyal and plays a key role in many battles.
  • Uryu Ishida: A Quincy, a race of spiritual warriors who oppose Hollows. Uryu is highly skilled with a bow and has a complex relationship with Ichigo as both rival and ally.
  • Yasutora "Chad" Sado: Ichigo’s close friend with a strong sense of justice. Chad discovers his own spiritual powers, which manifest as immense physical strength and protective abilities.
  • Sosuke Aizen: The primary antagonist in early arcs of the series. Once a respected Soul Reaper, Aizen’s betrayal and rise to power form a central conflict.
