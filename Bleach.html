Bleach

Bleach follows the story of Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who can see ghosts. His life is turned upside down when he inadvertently obtains the powers of a Soul Reaper, a death god tasked with defending the living from evil spirits known as Hollows. As Ichigo adjusts to his new role, he learns of dark secrets concerning the spirit world and divisions and hidden conflicts. In this series, Ichigo and his friends must battle very powerful enemies while protecting innocents and uncovering more about their real destinies. The series contains themes of friendship, duty, and sacrifice, as Ichigo and his allies face an endless number of challenges for the protection of the fine balance between the world of the living and the afterlife. Main Characters: Ichigo Kurosaki: The protagonist, Ichigo, is an obstinate and fearless teenager who becomes a Soul Reaper after inheriting powers from Rukia Kuchiki. His purpose is to protect his loved ones from spiritual threats and to uncover the mysteries of his own powers. Rukia Kuchiki: A Soul Reaper who gives Ichigo her powers originally. She plays a significant role in training Ichigo and is also a very close friend of his. Rukia comes from a noble family in the Soul Society. Orihime Inoue: A classmate of Ichigo's who possesses the ability to heal others by using spiritual powers. Orihime becomes an integral part of Ichigo's team, helping him during battles. Uryū Ishida: A Quincy, a human who can fight Hollows using bows and arrows, who becomes Ichigo's enemy in the beginning but later joins his side. Uryū is quite intelligent and an able fighter. Renji Abarai: Initially, he is a Soul Reaper who sees Ichigo as a rival; eventually, he turns out to become one of Ichigo's strongest allies. Very fiercely loyal and protective of his friends, Renji. Karin Kurosaki: Ichigo's younger sister, athletic and tough. Though she doesn't have the powers of a Soul Reaper, she plays a role in helping her brother. Yuzu Kurosaki: Ichigo's other younger sister. She is kind-hearted and tends to take care of the household. Like Karin, she doesn't know about the spiritual world but is affected by Ichigo's battles. Sōsuke Aizen: The main antagonist of the Soul Society arc, Aizen is a former captain of the Soul Reapers who betrays his comrades and seeks to overthrow the Soul King in a bid to reshape the world.
